Ingredients:
 - 1 1/4 cups of water
 - 4 1/2 cups of bread flour
 - 3 tbsp white sugar
 - 1 tsp salt
 - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
 - 1 tbsp instant yeast
 - 4 quarts of water

Directions:
 - Combine the 1 1/4 cup of water, flour, sugar, salt, oil, & yeast in a mixing bowl
 - Knead the dough until small portions can stretch without tearing
 - Tranfer dough to oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and towel, then let rise for 2 hours
 - Divide the dough into 6-8 pieces, roll each piece, connect the ends to form a circle
 - Preheat oven to 475 degrees and grease a baking sheet
 - Boil 4 quarts water in a large pot
 - Boil bagels 3-4 at a time until they rise to the surface (about 1 minute)
 - Remove bagels and place on baking sheet
 - Bake in the oven until the bagels turn golden brown (15-20 minutes)
