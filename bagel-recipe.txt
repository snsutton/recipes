Ingredients:
 - 1 1/4 cups of water
 - 4 1/2 cups of bread flour
 - 3 tbsp white sugar
 - 1 tsp salt
 - 2 tbsp vegetable oil
 - 1 tbsp instant yeast
 - 4 quarts of water

Directions:
 1. Combine the 1 1/4 cup of water, flour, sugar, salt, oil, & yeast in a mixing bowl
 2. Knead the dough until small portions can stretch without tearing
 3. Tranfer dough to oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap and towel
 4. Let dough rise for 2 hours
 5. Divide the dough into 6-8 pieces, roll each piece, connect the ends to form a circle
 6. Preheat oven to 475 degrees and grease a baking sheet
 7. Boil 4 quarts water in a large pot
 8. Boil bagels 3-4 at a time until they rise to the surface (about 1 minute)
 9. Remove bagels and place on baking sheet
 10.Bake in the oven until the bagels turn golden brown (15-20 minutes)
